The new Fiat Panda 4x4 has scooped its first major magazine award. The latest four-wheel drive version of Fiat's hugely popular Panda city car has just been named SUV of the Year in the prestigious 2012 Top Gear Awards. The award's judges put the new all-terrain Panda ahead of many very capable, larger - and far more expensive - dedicated SUV competitors, particularly praising its ride, comfort and off-road ability.
A 'giant-killing' achievement for this all-wheel drive equipped version of Fiat's eco-friendly city car.

  
  *TopGear magazine awards 2012.
